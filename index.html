About me

Welcome to my website!

I’m Unnati Tolani – a second-year MPA student at LSE specializing in Economic Policy. I’m passionate about creating real-world change through the power of analysis. My curiosity about how economic systems impact people’s lives led me to study Economics and Public Administration at the London School of Economics. I’ve worked at Accenture, improving client strategies with data, and at the World Bank, developing financial metrics for over 100 economies. My roles with NITI Aayog and the Bank of England, where I focus on AI strategy, have deepened my understanding of policy-making and sustainable growth. For me, it’s about using data to ask the real-world policy questions.
