CC8. Big Data. Extracting a story from millions of prices.

I analyzed UK market trends by processing data from the Long Run Prices Database (LRPD) to calculate average prices for fish-related products and monthly median prices for bananas. The results are visualized in bar charts, demonstrating price fluctuations and trends in these key food sectors.
